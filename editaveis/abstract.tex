The $\pi$col√© product consists of an autonomous vending machine aimed to make popsicle sales easier and cheaper in the long term. The costumer will only require to buy the desired flavors in an online website, pay with credit card and get the popsicles that will be release automatically. In addition, the product will be self-sustaining, as part of the energy used for the system will come from solar energy. Furthermore, the system will have a sophisticated design, making sales easier, thereby fulfilling it's market target. This paper presents an analysis of how the project will be executed and the reason of its choice.
